About SKV

The company traces its origins back to 2021, when SKTL was established with a primary focus on Furniture and Interior projects. Over time, we expanded our capabilities and diversified our operations, leading to our entry into the manufacturing of decorative glass in 2024. This expansion resulted in the formation of a new entity, SKV Glass & Interiors.

We operate a fully equipped manufacturing unit near IMT Manesar, Gurugram, featuring advanced machinery and modern infrastructure designed to deliver premium-quality decorative glass products. SKV Glass & Interiors is driven by a team of seasoned professionals with over 15 years of industry experience and in-depth product knowledge.
